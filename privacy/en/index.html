Privacy Policy

Weighloom is an offline weight-tracking app. Your entries are processed and stored only on your current device.

01

Data processed on your device

The app processes and stores the following data on your device:

  • Weight entries, including the recorded weight, weigh-in status, date, and time;
  • Height, target weight, weight unit, appearance, and language preferences;
  • Your daily reminder setting and reminder time;
  • Derived data calculated on-device for trends, statistics, and BMI reference results.

The current version does not create accounts or send this data to a developer-operated server.

02

How data is used

This data is used only to provide features that you choose to use:

  • Saving, editing, deleting, and displaying weight entries;
  • Generating offline charts and statistics by date;
  • Showing your current weight, target weight, and distance to target;
  • Calculating adult BMI reference results;
  • Scheduling local weigh-in reminders;
  • Exporting or restoring a local backup at your request.
03

Collection, sharing, and tracking

The current version does not collect weight or other personal data on developer-operated servers. It contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and the developer does not sell or share your data with third parties.

The app does not use advertising identifiers or track activity across apps or websites, so it does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

04

Notification permission

The app requests notification permission only when you turn on Daily Weigh-In Reminder. Reminders are scheduled on-device using Apple’s local notification system, are not sent through a developer-operated server, and never include your weight values.

You can turn reminders off in the app or change notification permission in iOS or iPadOS Settings at any time.

05

Local backups and file providers

When you export a backup, the app creates a JSON file containing weight entries and related settings, then asks you to choose where to save it. This file contains personal health-related information and should be stored securely.

The app never uploads backups automatically. If you save a backup to iCloud Drive or a third-party file provider, that service and its privacy policy govern subsequent processing. The app cannot control files that you choose to share or copy.

06

Retention and deletion

  • On-device entries remain until you delete an entry, use Delete All Weight Entries, or uninstall the app;
  • You can clear height and target weight in Settings and turn reminders off at any time;
  • Uninstalling the app normally removes data from its app sandbox;
  • You must manage or delete copies stored in exported files or device backups from their respective locations.

Because the current version has no accounts or developer-operated servers, the developer holds no cloud data associated with you.

07

System features and diagnostics

The current version uses only Apple-provided on-device storage, file selection, clipboard, and local notification features. Diagnostic information is copied to the clipboard only when you tap Copy Diagnostic Information. It includes the app version, system version, and device type, but no weight or other health data.

The app does not access HealthKit, location, camera, microphone, or the photo library. This privacy policy website uses no cookies, analytics scripts, or third-party resources.

08

BMI and health notice

BMI and weight statistics are general reference information for adults aged 18 and older. They are not appropriate for people under 18, people who are pregnant, or certain other groups. They do not constitute a medical diagnosis or treatment and are not dietary advice.

Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions based on these results.
09

Children

The current BMI reference feature is intended for adults aged 18 and older. The app does not use accounts, servers, advertising, or analytics SDKs to actively collect personal information from children.

10

Policy changes and contact

If the app’s data practices change, this policy and its update date will be revised. If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, HealthKit, analytics, advertising, or other online features, the relevant disclosures and permission requests will be updated before those features become available.

For privacy or data-handling questions, visit the Weighloom support page.
