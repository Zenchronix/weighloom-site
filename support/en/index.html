01

Entries and backups

Weight entries, notes, height, target weight, and water entries stay on your current device. The first-release build does not enable automatic CloudKit sync. Export a local backup from Settings before changing devices, uninstalling the app, or deleting entries, then restore it manually on the new device.

02

A reminder did not appear

Confirm that the relevant weigh-in, hydration, or weekly/monthly report reminder is enabled in the app, then check Weighloom’s notification permission in system Settings. Reminders are scheduled locally and do not require an internet connection. Reports are a Pro feature; basic hydration reminders are available to everyone.

03

Apple Health Active Calories

Active Calories reads Active Energy from Apple Health only. If no data appears, confirm that your device supports Apple Health, that Active Energy has been recorded for the day, and that Weighloom has read permission. The app never writes to Apple Health.

04

Pro purchases and restoration

Monthly and yearly plans renew automatically; Lifetime is a one-time purchase. Apple processes all payments. Open Weighloom Pro in Settings and tap Restore Purchases to restore an active entitlement for the same Apple Account. Manage or cancel subscriptions in the system Apple Account subscription settings.

05

Trends, BMI, and health information

You can add multiple entries per day; cross-day trends use the day’s last entry. BMI, weight trends, hydration progress, the personal fluctuation baseline, and food information are general wellness references and do not replace medical advice or diagnosis. See Health & Safety Information and Sources.
