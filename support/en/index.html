01

Entries and data

Your weight, height, target weight, and settings are stored only on your current device by default. The app has no accounts or cloud sync.

Export a local backup from Settings before changing devices, uninstalling the app, or deleting all entries.

02

A reminder did not appear

Confirm that Daily Weigh-In Reminder is enabled in the app, then check Weighloom’s notification permission in system Settings. Reminders use local notifications and do not require an internet connection.

03

Trends and analysis

You can add multiple entries on the same day. Cross-day trends and change analysis use the last entry of each day. BMI and weight statistics are general reference information and do not replace medical advice or diagnosis.

04

Contact support

If the issue remains unresolved, contact the developer through Weighloom’s public support-request channel.

Submit a support request

Do not include weight entries, backup files, identity information, or other sensitive personal data in a public request. Include the app version, system version, device type, and steps to reproduce the issue.
